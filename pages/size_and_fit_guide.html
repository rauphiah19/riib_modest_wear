How to Measure

Accurate measurements are essential for finding your perfect modest wear fit. Follow our step-by-step guide for precise sizing.

Pro Tip

For most accurate measurements, wear fitted undergarments and ask someone to help you measure. Keep the tape measure parallel to the floor.

1

Bust Measurement

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape measure parallel to the floor. For modest wear, we recommend adding 2-4 inches for comfortable, non-clingy fit.

Key for abayas and tunics
2

Waist Measurement

Measure around your natural waistline (typically the narrowest part of your torso). For modest wear with loose fits, this helps determine proper draping and silhouette.

Important for fitted waist styles
3

Hip Measurement

Measure around the fullest part of your hips and buttocks. This ensures proper coverage and comfortable movement in modest garments.

Essential for full-length garments
4

Shoulder Width

Measure from shoulder point to shoulder point across your back. This ensures proper fit across shoulders for modest coverage and comfortable movement.

Critical for proper draping
5

Length Measurements

Measure from shoulder to desired hem length. For modest wear, we offer various lengths to ensure appropriate coverage based on your preferences.

Customizable for your height

Maintaining Your Perfect Fit

Proper care ensures your RIIB garments maintain their shape, fit, and modest silhouette over time.

Washing Instructions

  • Machine wash cold with similar colors
  • Use gentle cycle for delicate fabrics
  • Avoid bleach and harsh detergents
  • Turn embroidered pieces inside out

Drying & Storage

  • Air dry flat or hang to maintain shape
  • Avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading
  • Iron on low heat while slightly damp
  • Store on padded hangers to preserve drape

Important Note

Following these care instructions helps maintain the modest fit and elegant drape of your RIIB garments. Improper care may affect sizing and silhouette over time.
